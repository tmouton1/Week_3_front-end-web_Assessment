The Greatest Journey Is Along The Path To Self

Washington, D.C. native Sihnuu Hetep started her yoga journey in 2008 with Ausarian Master Teacher Kwesi Karamoko. She is well versed in Vinyasa Yoga, Power Yoga, Restorative Yoga, Yin Yoga and Yoga catering to Trauma sensitive and special populations. In 2015 she took her understanding of yoga and meditation to the next level while studying for her 500 hour in Yoga Therapy and Stress Transformation. She is currently diving deep into coding, which will allow her to navigate the tech world from a wellness lens. It is her mission to share the healing power going inward with her loved ones and community.

My Vision

With tech and wellness combined, my goal is to position myself as community leader, build customized virutal wellness solutions, and reach a wider audience.
